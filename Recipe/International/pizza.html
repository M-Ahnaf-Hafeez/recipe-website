Pizza

Ingredients

  • For the Pizza Dough:
    • 2 ¼ tsp (1 packet) active dry yeast
    • 1 ½ cups warm water (110°F or 45°C)
    • 3 ½ - 4 cups all-purpose flour
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 tsp salt
    • 1 tsp sugar
  • For the Pizza Sauce:
    • 1 cup tomato sauce
    • 1 tbsp tomato paste
    • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
    • 1 tsp dried oregano
    • 1 tsp dried basil
    • 1 tsp olive oil
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • For the Toppings:
    • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    • 1 ½ cups sliced pepperoni (or other preferred meat/veggies)
    • 1 cup sliced bell peppers
    • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
    • ½ cup sliced olives
    • Fresh basil leaves (optional)
    • Red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions

  • Preparing the Dough:
    • In a large mixing bowl, combine warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy.
    • Add olive oil, salt, and 2 cups of flour to the yeast mixture. Stir to combine. Gradually add the remaining flour until a dough forms.
    • Turn the dough onto a floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
    • Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours, or until it doubles in size.
  • Making the Sauce:
    • In a small bowl, mix tomato sauce, tomato paste, minced garlic, oregano, basil, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  • Assembling the Pizza:
    • Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up.
    • Punch down the dough and divide it into two equal parts. Roll each part into a 12-inch circle on a floured surface.
    • Spread the pizza sauce evenly over each dough circle.
    • Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese and add your favorite toppings (pepperoni, bell peppers, mushrooms, olives, etc.).
  • Baking:
    • Transfer the pizzas to a pizza stone or a baking sheet. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  • Serving:
    • Garnish with fresh basil leaves and red pepper flakes if desired.
    • Slice and serve hot.
