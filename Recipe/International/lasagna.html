Classic Italian Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Meat Sauce:
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 lb ground beef
    • ½ lb Italian sausage (optional)
    • 1 onion, chopped
    • 3 garlic cloves, minced
    • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
    • 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
    • 2 tbsp tomato paste
    • ½ cup red wine (optional)
    • 2 tsp dried basil
    • 1 tsp dried oregano
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cheese Mixture:
    • 2 cups ricotta cheese
    • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • 1 egg
    • ¼ cup chopped parsley
  • Assembly:
    • 12 lasagna noodles
    • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  • Meat Sauce:
    • Cook beef and sausage in olive oil until browned. Add onion and garlic; cook until soft.
    • Add tomatoes, sauce, paste, wine, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  • Cheese Mixture:
    • Mix ricotta, Parmesan, egg, and parsley in a bowl.
  • Noodles:
    • Cook noodles per package instructions.
  • Assemble:
    • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Spread meat sauce in a 9x13-inch dish.
    • Layer with noodles, ricotta mix, mozzarella, and sauce. Repeat layers, ending with mozzarella.
  • Bake:
    • Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Uncover and bake for 25 more minutes.
    • Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
