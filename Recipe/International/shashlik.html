Chicken Shashlik with Rice

Ingredients

  • For the Chicken Shashlik:
    • 1 kg chicken breast (cut into cubes)
    • 1 large onion (cut into chunks)
    • 1 large green bell pepper (cut into chunks)
    • 1 large red bell pepper (cut into chunks)
    • 1 large tomato (cut into chunks)
    • 2 tbsp soy sauce
    • 2 tbsp vinegar
    • 2 tbsp ketchup
    • 2 tbsp chili sauce
    • 1 tsp garlic paste
    • 1 tsp ginger paste
    • 1 tsp red chili powder
    • 1 tsp black pepper
    • Salt to taste
    • 2 tbsp oil
  • For the Shashlik Sauce:
    • 1 cup ketchup
    • 2 tbsp soy sauce
    • 2 tbsp vinegar
    • 1 tbsp chili sauce
    • 1 tbsp sugar
    • 1 tsp garlic paste
    • 1 tsp ginger paste
    • 1 tsp red chili powder
    • 1 tsp black pepper
    • Salt to taste
    • ½ cup water
  • For the Rice:
    • 2 cups basmati rice
    • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
    • 1 tsp cumin seeds
    • 1 bay leaf
    • 2-3 cloves
    • 1 cinnamon stick
    • 4 cups water
    • 2 tbsp oil
    • Salt to taste

Instructions

  • Marinating the Chicken:
    • Combine soy sauce, vinegar, ketchup, chili sauce, garlic paste, ginger paste, red chili powder, black pepper, salt, and oil in a bowl.
    • Add chicken cubes and mix well. Marinate for at least 1 hour.
  • Preparing the Skewers:
    • Thread chicken, onion, bell peppers, and tomato alternately onto skewers.
    • Grill the skewers on medium heat until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred, about 10-15 minutes. You can also cook in a preheated oven at 200°C (390°F) for about 20 minutes or pan-fry until done.
  • Making the Shashlik Sauce:
    • Combine all sauce ingredients in a small saucepan.
    • Cook over medium heat until the sauce thickens, stirring occasionally. Adjust seasoning as needed.
  • Preparing the Rice:
    • Heat oil in a pot and fry the chopped onion until golden brown.
    • Add cumin seeds, bay leaf, cloves, and cinnamon stick. Fry for a few seconds.
    • Add washed and drained rice, and fry for 2-3 minutes. Add water and salt. Bring to a boil, then cover and cook on low heat until the rice is tender and water is absorbed (about 15-20 minutes).
  • Serving:
    • Serve the chicken shashlik skewers over a bed of rice.
    • Drizzle shashlik sauce over the skewers or serve on the side.
